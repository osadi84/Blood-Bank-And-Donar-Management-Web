Frequently Asked Questions

You can register as a blood donor by visiting our registration page and filling out the online form. The process takes about 5 minutes. Once registered, you'll be added to our donor database and can be contacted during emergencies.

Our average emergency response time is 45 minutes. We use GPS technology to locate the nearest compatible donors and send immediate alerts. In critical cases, we can mobilize donors within 30 minutes.

Yes, we welcome hospital partnerships. Hospitals can integrate with our system to access our donor network, manage inventory, and use our emergency alert system. Contact our partnership team for more information.
