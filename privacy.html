Privacy Policy

LifeFlow Blood Bank & Donor Management System

1. Introduction

LifeFlow is committed to protecting the privacy of donors, hospitals, and users. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Full name, contact number, email
  • Blood group and medical details
  • Location data (for donor matching)
  • Donation history

3. How We Use Information

  • To connect donors with hospitals
  • To manage blood inventory
  • To send emergency alerts
  • To organize donation campaigns

4. Data Security

We use secure servers and encrypted communication methods to protect sensitive medical and personal information.

5. Sharing Information

Personal information is shared only with authorized hospitals and emergency services when required.

6. User Rights

Users can update or delete their data by contacting LifeFlow support.
