Terms and Conditions
1. Eligibility

You must be at least 18 years old and meet all health requirements to register as a blood donor.

2. Data Usage

Your personal and health information will be used solely for blood donation purposes and emergency coordination.

3. Donor Responsibilities

As a registered donor, you agree to provide accurate health information and update your availability status regularly.

4. Emergency Contact

You consent to be contacted during emergency blood requirements via phone, SMS, or email.

5. Privacy

Your information will be protected according to Sri Lankan data protection laws and will not be shared with third parties.

Privacy Policy
1. Information Collection

We collect personal information, health data, and location information necessary for blood donation coordination.

2. Data Protection

All data is encrypted and stored securely. Access is restricted to authorized personnel only.

3. Usage Purpose

Your data is used for donor matching, emergency alerts, health tracking, and system improvement.

4. Data Sharing

We only share necessary information with hospitals during emergency blood requests.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time.
