Our Journey

Building a Smarter Blood Management System

LifeFlow is a newly developed digital platform created by Health ICT students to modernize blood donation and emergency blood management processes in Sri Lanka.

The project focuses on improving donor coordination, reducing response times, and ensuring secure and accurate data handling through technology-driven solutions.
